Hello! Welcome to my website. This is a website in which I have made to answer your most demanding questions about mental health. Including how to keep it well, such as how to preserve it and how to prevent it from deteriorating due to external events; Its nature, really looking into what it is; its determinants, what can determine of one may result with an ill mental health; and finally, the symptoms of a poor mental health.

This was a fun project for Digital Design. I had to engage my passion for frontend development in order to create a cohesive and nice-looking product to present to you all. Essentially what I had to learn was this is I need to plan my documents better, how to ask others for ideas, and to do qualitative analysis on my UI/UX design.
